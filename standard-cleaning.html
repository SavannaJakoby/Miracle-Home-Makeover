Standard / Routine Cleaning

Consistent, Reliable Cleaning for a Fresh & Comfortable Space

My Standard Routine Cleaning is designed to maintain a clean, organized, and comfortable environment through regular upkeep. This package focuses on essential cleaning tasks to keep your home or business looking its best through your day-to-day life.

General Cleaning (All Areas)

  • Dusting all accessible surfaces (furniture, shelves, décor)
  • Wiping down countertops and flat surfaces
  • Vacuuming carpets and rugs
  • Sweeping and mopping hard floors
  • Emptying trash and replacing liners
  • Light tidying and straightening

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Wipe down countertops and backsplashes
  • Clean exterior of appliances (stove, fridge, microwave, dishwasher)
  • Clean and sanitize sink and faucet
  • Wipe cabinet fronts
  • Spot clean walls and high-touch areas

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Clean and disinfect toilets
  • Scrub sinks, counters, and faucets
  • Clean tubs and showers (light buildup removal)
  • Wipe mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Sanitize high-touch areas

Bedroom Cleaning

  • Dust furniture and surfaces
  • Make beds (linen change optional)
  • Vacuum floors and rugs
  • Empty trash

Living Areas & Common Spaces

  • Dust and wipe furniture
  • Light upholstery vacuuming
  • Clean floors (vacuum/mop)
  • Straighten pillows, blankets, and décor

Service Frequency Options

  • Weekly
  • Bi-weekly
  • Monthly
  • Custom schedules available

Not Included (Add-On Services)

  • Deep cleaning (baseboards, heavy buildup, inside appliances)
  • Interior cabinet cleaning
  • Carpet shampooing
  • Exterior window cleaning
  • Post-construction cleaning

Ideal For

  • Busy families
  • Working professionals
  • Seniors
  • Offices needing light maintenance
  • Anyone wanting a consistently clean space
