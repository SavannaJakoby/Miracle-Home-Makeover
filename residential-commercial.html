Residential & Commercial Cleaning

Residential Cleaning

I focus on maintaining a spotless living environment for homeowners, renters, and families. My services are customized to fit the needs of each home—whether it’s routine upkeep or a full deep clean.

Residential cleaning is designed to improve comfort, hygiene, and peace of mind while saving you time and effort. Your home won’t just look clean—it will truly feel clean.

General Cleaning Tasks

  • Dusting furniture, shelves, and décor
  • Vacuuming carpets, rugs, and upholstery
  • Sweeping and mopping floors
  • Emptying trash and replacing liners
  • Making beds and general tidying

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Cleaning countertops, backsplashes, and cabinets
  • Wiping down appliances (microwave, stove, refrigerator exterior)
  • Scrubbing sinks and faucets
  • Degreasing surfaces and removing food buildup

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Disinfecting toilets, tubs, showers, and sinks
  • Cleaning mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Removing soap scum, mold, and mildew
  • Sanitizing high-touch areas

Deep Cleaning (Optional Add-On)

  • Baseboards, doors, ceiling fans, light fixtures, and blinds
  • Behind and under furniture
  • Inside kitchen appliances (oven, refrigerator)
  • Detailed scrubbing of grout and buildup areas

Commercial Cleaning

I provide cleaning services designed to maintain clean, safe, and professional environments for businesses, employees, and customers. Services can be scheduled regularly and tailored to your space.

Commercial cleaning helps maintain a professional image, ensures health and safety standards, and creates a better environment for both staff and customers.

General Facility Cleaning

  • Dusting and wiping desks, counters, and surfaces
  • Vacuuming carpets and cleaning floors
  • Trash removal and recycling
  • Cleaning entryways, hallways, and common areas

Restroom Sanitation

  • Disinfecting toilets, urinals, sinks, and partitions
  • Restocking paper products and soap
  • Sanitizing high-touch surfaces
  • Odor control and deep sanitation

Office & Workspace Cleaning

  • Cleaning workstations, conference rooms, and breakrooms
  • Sanitizing shared equipment (phones, keyboards, door handles)
  • Maintaining a clean, organized environment

Retail & Customer Areas

  • Cleaning display areas, shelves, and floors
  • Glass and window cleaning
  • Keeping high-traffic areas spotless

Janitorial Services

  • Daily, weekly, or custom cleaning schedules

Specialized Services

  • Medical office cleaning
  • Post-construction commercial cleanup
  • Organization and decluttering
  • Seasonal or spring cleaning
  • Pre/Post party clean-up
