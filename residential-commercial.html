Residential & Commercial Cleaning

Residential Cleaning

Focuses on maintaining a spotless living environment for homeowners, renters, and families. These services are customized to fit the needs of each home, whether it’s routine upkeep or a major deep clean.

Residential cleaning is designed to improve comfort, hygiene, and peace of mind while saving homeowners time and effort. It ensures your home not only looks clean—but truly feels clean.

General Cleaning Tasks

  • Dusting furniture, shelves, and décor
  • Vacuuming carpets, rugs, and upholstery
  • Sweeping and mopping floors
  • Emptying trash and replacing liners
  • Making beds and general tidying

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Cleaning countertops, backsplashes, and cabinets
  • Wiping down appliances (microwave, stove, refrigerator exterior)
  • Scrubbing sinks and faucets
  • Degreasing surfaces and removing food buildup

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Disinfecting toilets, tubs, showers, and sinks
  • Cleaning mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Removing soap scum, mold, and mildew
  • Sanitizing high-touch areas

Deep Cleaning (Optional Add-On)

  • Baseboards, doors, ceiling fans/light fixtures, and blinds
  • Behind and under furniture
  • Inside kitchen appliances (oven, refrigerator)
  • Detailed scrubbing of grout and buildup areas

Commercial Cleaning

Focuses on maintaining clean, safe, and professional environments for businesses, employees, and customers. These services are often scheduled regularly and tailored to the size and type of facility.

Commercial cleaning helps businesses maintain a professional image, ensure health and safety compliance, and create a positive environment.

General Facility Cleaning

  • Dusting and wiping desks, counters, and surfaces
  • Vacuuming carpets and cleaning floors
  • Trash removal and recycling
  • Cleaning entryways, hallways, and common areas

Restroom Sanitation

  • Disinfecting toilets, sinks, and partitions
  • Restocking supplies
  • Sanitizing high-touch surfaces
  • Odor control

Office & Workspace Cleaning

  • Cleaning workstations and breakrooms
  • Sanitizing shared equipment
  • Maintaining a clean environment

Retail & Customer Areas

  • Cleaning display areas and floors
  • Glass and window cleaning
  • Keeping high-traffic areas spotless

Janitorial Services

  • Daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly or custom schedules

Specialized Services

  • Medical office cleaning
  • Post-construction cleanup
  • Organization / decluttering
  • Seasonal or spring cleaning
  • Pre/Post party clean-up
