Post-Construction Cleaning Services

My post-construction cleaning service is designed to remove dust, debris, and construction residue left behind after building, remodeling, or renovation projects. I transform newly built or renovated spaces into clean, safe, and ready-to-use environments.

What’s Included

Rough Cleaning (Initial Phase)

  • Removal of large debris, trash, and leftover materials
  • Sweeping and vacuuming to eliminate heavy dust
  • Preparing the space for detailed cleaning

Detailed Cleaning

  • Dusting all surfaces including walls, ceilings, and fixtures
  • Cleaning baseboards, trim, doors, and frames
  • Removing fine dust from vents, light fixtures, and corners
  • Wiping down all surfaces to remove residue

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Cleaning inside and outside of cabinets and drawers
  • Wiping down countertops and backsplashes
  • Cleaning appliances (interior and exterior)
  • Removing construction dust and adhesive residue

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Deep cleaning and sanitizing toilets, tubs, showers, and sinks
  • Removing grout haze, dust, and residue from tile surfaces
  • Cleaning mirrors, fixtures, and glass

Window & Glass Cleaning

  • Removing stickers, tape, paint splatter, and debris
  • Cleaning interior windows, frames, and tracks
  • Polishing glass surfaces for a streak-free finish

Floor Cleaning & Finishing

  • Sweeping, vacuuming, and mopping all floors
  • Removing dust from corners and edges
  • Polishing or detailing floors as needed

Final Touch Cleaning (Final Phase)

  • Detailed inspection and touch-ups
  • Removing any remaining dust or smudges
  • Ensuring the space is spotless and presentation-ready

Areas I Service

  • Newly built homes
  • Renovated houses and apartments
  • Commercial buildings and offices
  • Retail spaces and restaurants
  • Construction sites and remodel projects

Optional Add-Ons

  • Carpet and upholstery cleaning
  • High dusting (ceilings, beams, tall fixtures - equipment provided)

Ideal For

  • Contractors and builders
  • Homeowners after renovations
  • Property developers
  • Real estate professionals preparing listings
