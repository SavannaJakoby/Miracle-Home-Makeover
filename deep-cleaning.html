Deep Cleaning

Detailed. Thorough. Top-to-Bottom Cleaning You Can Trust.

Deep cleaning goes beyond regular maintenance by targeting hidden dirt, buildup, and hard-to-reach areas to restore your space to a like-new condition.

Standard Deep Clean

Perfect for homes that need a refresh

  • Kitchen detailing including cabinet faces, cabinet tops, and appliance exteriors
  • Interior appliance cleaning (microwave, oven, refrigerator)
  • Scrubbing sink, stovetop, backsplash, and countertops
  • Bathroom sanitation (toilets, tubs, showers, sinks)
  • Polishing bathroom cabinets and mirrors
  • Detailed dusting (baseboards, blinds, furniture)
  • Light fixture and ceiling fan dusting
  • Interior window cleaning (accessible areas)
  • Glass and window shining
  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors (including stairs)
  • Trash removal

Best For: First-time clients, seasonal cleaning

Premium Deep Clean

More detailed attention for heavily used spaces

Includes everything in Standard PLUS:

  • Cabinet interior wipe-down
  • Door and vent cleaning
  • Heavy grease and grime removal
  • Detailed tile and grout scrubbing
  • Spot cleaning walls and high-touch areas

Best For: Homes needing extra care or long gaps between cleanings

Ultimate Deep Clean

Top-to-bottom, no-detail-missed cleaning

Includes everything in Premium PLUS:

  • Behind and under furniture/appliances (if accessible)
  • Hand-scrubbed baseboards throughout
  • Upholstery vacuuming
  • Closet and pantry wipe-down (upon request)
  • Extra attention to buildup, stains, and neglected areas

Best For: Move-ins, move-outs, extreme deep cleaning
