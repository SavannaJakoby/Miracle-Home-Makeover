Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Leave It Spotless. Start Fresh.

My Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning service provides a thorough, top-to-bottom deep clean designed to prepare a home for new occupants or ensure it’s left in pristine condition. Whether you’re moving in or moving out, I handle the cleaning so you can focus on your transition.

Full Home Deep Cleaning

  • Dusting all surfaces, baseboards, ceiling fans, and light fixtures
  • Cleaning doors, vents, and light switches
  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors
  • Removing cobwebs and buildup in corners

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Clean inside and outside of cabinets and drawers
  • Deep clean countertops and backsplashes
  • Clean inside and outside of appliances:
    • Refrigerator
    • Oven & stovetop
    • Microwave
  • Scrub and sanitize sink and faucet
  • Remove grease, food residue, and buildup

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Deep scrub and disinfect toilets, tubs, and showers
  • Clean sinks, counters, and fixtures
  • Remove soap scum, mildew, and hard water stains
  • Clean mirrors and glass
  • Sanitize all high-touch areas

Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • Clean closets, shelves, and storage spaces
  • Dust and wipe all surfaces
  • Vacuum carpets and clean floors
  • Clean interior windows, sills, and blinds

Optional Add-Ons

  • Carpet shampooing
  • Wall washing (full surface)
  • Baseboards hand-scrubbed throughout
  • Garage or basement cleaning

Ideal For

  • Renters looking to secure deposits
  • Homeowners buying or selling
  • Property managers and landlords
  • Real estate staging and showings
