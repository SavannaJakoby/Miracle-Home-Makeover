Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Leave It Spotless. Start Fresh.

My Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning service provides a thorough, top-to-bottom deep clean designed to prepare a home for new occupants or ensure it’s left in pristine condition.

Full Home Deep Cleaning

  • Dusting all surfaces, baseboards, ceiling fans, and light fixtures
  • Cleaning doors, vents, and light switches
  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors
  • Removing cobwebs and buildup

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Clean inside and outside of cabinets and drawers
  • Deep clean countertops and backsplashes
  • Clean appliances (fridge, oven, microwave)
  • Scrub and sanitize sink
  • Remove grease and buildup

Bathroom Cleaning

  • Deep scrub toilets, tubs, and showers
  • Clean sinks and fixtures
  • Remove soap scum and stains
  • Clean mirrors and glass
  • Sanitize high-touch areas

Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • Clean closets and storage spaces
  • Dust and wipe surfaces
  • Vacuum carpets and floors
  • Clean windows and blinds

Optional Add-Ons

  • Carpet shampooing
  • Wall washing
  • Baseboards hand-scrubbed
  • Garage or basement cleaning

Ideal For

  • Renters securing deposits
  • Homeowners buying or selling
  • Landlords and property managers
  • Real estate showings
